Some of our best works

Blind Day

Blind Day is a short film, which takes you closer to the life of a blind beggar. He sings the same tune everyday, to which people sympathise and give him money. This is his easy going lifestyle.

One fine day, the blind beggar is challenged by a balloon seller, to find a way of earning instead of begging. The blind beggar debates on how a blind man can earn money and manage his livelihood.

The balloon seller calls him a hypocrite and goes to sell balloons, which was his chosen profession. The blind beggar, by way of his little daughter, discovers that the balloon seller was also blind and laughs.

Zidd

Zidd is a music video, inspired by a real life teenage love story. It takes you on a romantic journey, as the guy watches two kids - a boy and girl playing in the park with each other, and remembers his lady love.

While playing, the kids have a fight and the girl becomes angry. The boy says sorry to her, and the fight is resolved on a moment. The guy smiles at them, but when he looks at his life, his smile vanishes.

His lady love has left him forever, because of his mistake.

PVC Shaheed Captain Manoj Pandey Documentary

Lucknow is home to a patriot, a martyr and a national hero, Captain Manoj Pandey. He sacrificed his life in the Kargil War and was awarded the Param Vir Chakra for his bravery.

The Shaheed Captain Manoj Pandey Documentary was produced on the occasion of Independence Day by Filming Eye. It highlights the life and achievements of Shaheed Captain Manoj Pandey in words of his family, who lives in Lucknow.

He is the pride of our nation and our city as well. This documentary is an effort to bring his virtues before the world.
